Asset and Machine Monitoring

Monitor machines and assets, control real-time performance, reduce
energy costs, minimize downtime, and manage maintenance.

Request a Demo
It enables you to..
Comprehensive Asset Oversight
  • Gain visibility into the status and performance of all your assets, whether old or new, primary or auxiliary.
  • Achieve a centralized view of your entire asset portfolio, simplifying management and decision-making.
Enhanced Efficiency and Cost Control
  • EMS contributes to a reduced carbon footprint and a more environmentally friendly operation.
  • It aligns with sustainability goals and may lead to certifications or compilance with green energy standards.
Roactive Maintenance Management
  • Implement condition-based maintenance strategies with confidence.
  • Stay on top of maintenance schedules and ensure machinery is serviced when needed, minimizing downtime and repair costs.
Immediate Response to Alerts
  • Receive instant notifications when machines exceed predefined thresholds or encounter alarms.
  • Take quick and informed action to address issues promptly, preventing potential disruptions and preserving productivity.
How it works for a factory:
  • Remote Asset Connectivity
    Connect to existing assets remotely, overcoming the challenge of continuous monitoring.
  • Customizable Alerts
    Configure complex alarms with condition types, boundary limits, and actions, with email and SMS distribution rules for rapid response.
  • Performance Dashboard
    Access real-time data for each monitored asset, including detailed health status, open alarms, and performance readings, with drill-down capabilities.
  • Maintenance Efficiency
    Enable maintenance teams to define default time windows for analysis, acknowledge alarms, and access condition status for effective troubleshooting.
  • Performance Improvements
    Implementing this application can lead to significant gains, including throughput increases of 5-20%, 20-30% reduction in unplanned downtime, and 2-13% energy consumption reduction.
  • Deployment Flexibility
    The application is available for deployment in your private or public cloud, ensuring scalability and accessibility.
  • Mission-Critical Uptime
    Ensure assets are operational, reducing events with unplanned downtime, higher scrap, and rework.
  • Cost Reduction
    Proactive maintenance and issue resolution lead to lower maintenance costs and unnecessary capital expenditures.
  • Enhanced Asset Utilization
    Achieve higher overall asset utilization, minimizing operational inefficiencies.
  • Data-Driven Insights
    Utilize data trending and analysis tools for informed decision-making and root cause analysis.
  • Transformative Results
    Companies implementing this application have witnessed significant improvements in their factory operations, translating into higher efficiency and cost savings.
